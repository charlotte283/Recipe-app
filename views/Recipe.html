Chocolate Chip Cookies

Classic chewy and gooey cookies filled with chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix together dry ingredients (flour, baking soda, salt).
  3. Cream together butter, sugars, and vanilla.
  4. Add eggs to the creamed mixture and beat well.
  5. Gradually combine dry ingredients with the wet mixture.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop dough onto baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  8. Let cool before serving.
